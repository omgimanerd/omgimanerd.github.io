Klick
Klick is a browser-based game made by me entirely with JavaScript and SVG. To play, prevent the red ball from hitting any other ball. The red ball will always move towards your mouse cursor while the other balls are governed by the laws of physics. Green balls have normal gravity but blue balls have reverse gravity. Currently, Klick is only supported by Google Chrome and Chromium-based browsers.
